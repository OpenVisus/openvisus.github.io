Examples

See OpenVisus in action with real scientific datasets. Many of these are powered by the National Science Data Fabric (NSDF), a collaboration with the University of Utah's SCI Institute.

IEEE SciVis Contest 2026

Visualizing the future of climate science with petascale oceanic and atmospheric data. The contest datasets are provided in IDX format, enabling efficient multi-resolution access and interactive visualization with OpenVisus.

Contest Website Launch Binder Cookbook Guide

$1,000 cash prize for the best submission. Use OpenVisus, Openvisuspy, and other open-source tools. Data hosted in the cloud.
