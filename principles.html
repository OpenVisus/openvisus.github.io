OpenVisus

Principles

The Core Values of the OpenViSUS Ecosystem. Our ecosystem is guided by principles that prioritize scientific impact, inclusivity, and sustainability:

Democratized Access

We believe visualization of massive data should not be tied to specialized, expensive hardware. Our tools are designed to be lightweight, scalable, and accessible to anyone, from field researchers with laptops to High-Performance Computing (HPC) centers.

FAIR Data Advocacy

We support the Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable (FAIR) data principles. We are committed to ensuring that data provenance is tracked and that visualization dashboards can be treated as persistent, citable research artifacts.

Open Collaboration

OpenViSUS is released under the OSI-approved BSD-3 license. We are transitioning from a single-lab project to a community-governed ecosystem, managed by an elected Leadership Council. We believe in building tools that act as an invisible acceleration layer for the scientific software you already use.

Respect and Safety

We are dedicated to a harassment-free experience for everyone. We strictly adhere to the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct in all our community spaces (GitHub, Zulip, and user groups) to ensure a welcoming environment regardless of experience level or background.
