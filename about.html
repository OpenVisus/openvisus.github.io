OpenVisus

About

The mission of OpenVisus is to provide support for the scientific community with Big Data management, analysis, and visualization tools. In this website we provide access to open-source software tools and libraries such as the ViSUS framework and the PIDX library. These softwares are distributed under the permissive BSD-3 license [Source 205].

Democratizing Access to Massive Scientific Data

OpenViSUS is an open-source software ecosystem designed for the fast reading, writing, and visualization of massive, multidimensional gridded datasets (terascale and petascale) [Source 49, 188]. Originally developed at the Scientific Computing and Imaging (SCI) Institute at the University of Utah [Source 198], OpenViSUS eliminates the data movement bottlenecks that encumber scientific discovery.

At its core is the IDX format, a cache-oblivious data layout that uses space-filling curves to enable efficient, progressive streaming [Source 50, 169]. This allows users to interactively explore and analyze massive datasets on commodity hardware—like a standard laptop—without needing to downsample or transfer the entire file [Source 50, 168].

Today, OpenViSUS is supported by a coalition of partners including ViSUS LLC, the San Diego Supercomputer Center (SDSC), and Open Tech Strategies [Source 49]. It acts as critical infrastructure for a thriving community of early adopters, including Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory (LLNL), NASA, NEON/Battelle, and the Cornell High Energy Synchrotron Source (CHESS) [Source 50].

Key Features

OpenVisus enables interactive exploration of petabyte-scale data on devices from supercomputers to commodity laptops, making big data accessible to all users.

📦

Efficient Data Storage

The IDX format stores data in hierarchical Z (HZ) order for cache-oblivious, progressive access. Organize data for efficient multi-resolution queries and scalable visualization.

📊

Scalable Visualization

Interactive visualization of terabyte and petabyte datasets without requiring high-end hardware. Start with coarse overviews and refine to full detail as needed.

⚡

Progressive Streaming

Optimized network utilization with state-of-the-art compression algorithms for fast data delivery and streaming from local storage or cloud repositories.

🖥️

Web-Based Dashboards

Customizable dashboards for data analysis accessible from any device with an internet connection. Cross-platform support for supercomputers, desktops, and laptops.
