Privacy Policy — OpenVisus Ecosystem & Hosted Services

Updated Date: February 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the OpenVisus open-source ecosystem (openvisus.org), our open-source software, and affiliated hosted services including the Scientist Cloud Data Portal and Scientist Cloud Storage (provided in partnership with ViSOAR LLC). Contact us at support@visoar.com with any questions.

1. You Own Your Data

Everything you upload to our hosted services (such as ScientistCloud) or process locally using OpenVisus is yours. We do not claim ownership of your scientific datasets, raw images, or proprietary experimental data.

  • No Selling or Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or project data with third parties unless you explicitly authorize us to do so, or the law requires it.
  • No AI Training Without Consent: Your private data is not used for AI training or personalized advertising without your explicit consent.

2. Open Science and Public Data

We actively support open science and public domain datasets. If you explicitly choose to share the data files you upload publicly (e.g., via a citable dashboard), they may be freely viewed and reused. Sharing public data does not override your personal account data privacy guarantees.

3. Data Collection, Cookies, and Logs

  • Website & Community: openvisus.org collects minimal personal information. We use standard cookies and server logs to keep the site running securely and to understand basic usage analytics. If you use our contact forms (via Formspree), we collect your name and email solely to respond to your inquiry.
  • GitHub: If you interact with our open-source repositories, your public contributions, username, and associated email are governed by GitHub's privacy terms.

4. Third-Party Integrations and Google Data

When you log into our hosted portals, you may use Auth0, an institutional LDAP login, or sign in with Google. We only request the minimum necessary access for functionality:

  • Google API Scopes Requested: openid, email, profile, https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file, and https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.send.
  • How Google Data is Used: These scopes are used exclusively for authentication, accessing Google Drive files only as selected by you, and sending email notifications that you initiate. Users utilize a picker UI to select specific files or folders for conversion (e.g., TIFF, NEXUS, HDF5 to OpenVisus IDX). Only these authorized files are accessed.
  • Revocation: You may revoke access to your Google account at any time via your Google Account Permissions settings.

5. Data Security

We use industry‑standard measures to keep your data safe:

  • Data Encryption: All user passwords and personal information are encrypted using strong standards (e.g., AES-256) both at rest and during transmission (TLS/SSL).
  • Access Controls: Access to sensitive data is limited to authorized personnel, protected by role-based access controls and two-factor authentication. Data that is not marked public requires authentication to access.
  • Audit and Monitoring: All access to sensitive information is logged, monitored, and regularly reviewed for suspicious activity.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You can add, manage, or delete your data at any time.

  • Account Deletion: We retain your data (including authorized Google data) only while your account is active. If you disconnect your account or request deletion, all associated user data is deleted from our servers within 30 days, unless legally required to retain it.
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymous usage data is retained for analytics purposes for up to 12 months.
  • How to Request Deletion: You may request the deletion of all data associated with your account by emailing support@visoar.com.

7. Compliance and Breach Notification

We regularly review our practices for compliance with applicable privacy and data protection regulations. In the event of a data breach impacting your information, you will be notified as required by applicable law, and mitigation steps will be communicated promptly.
