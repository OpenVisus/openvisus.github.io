OpenVisus

Roadmap

Where We Are Going (Will, Won't, Might). As part of our NSF POSE Phase I initiative, our community-driven roadmap is focused on stability, integration, and ecosystem governance:

What We Will Do (Near Term)

  • Governance: Establish a formal Leadership Council with defined term limits and publish clear Contribution Guidelines
  • Integration: Release stable plugins for existing tools
  • Deployment: Finalize our containerized local deployment solutions (Docker/Kubernetes) for secure environments .

What We Might Do (Medium Term)

  • Metadata & Provenance: Improve interoperability by creating robust tools to extract and attach experimental metadata to the IDX format.
  • 1D Data: Expand progressive streaming capabilities explicitly for massive 1D time-series datasets (e.g., cosmology and astrophysics).
  • FAIR Dashboards: Automate the assignment of Persistent Identifiers (DOIs) to specific dashboard configurations for long-term reproducibility.

What We Won't Do

  • We will not force users into proprietary silos or charge for access to the core open-source framework.
