OpenVisus

Community

OpenVisus powers collaborations across the National Science Data Fabric (NSDF) and beyond, enabling democratized access to large-scale scientific data.

NSDF & CHESS Cornell Synchrotron

NSDF and CHESS Collaborate to Democratize Data-Driven Scientific Discovery

National Science Data Fabric and the Cornell High Energy Synchrotron Source (CHESS) partnered to enable real-time visualization of large 3D datasets. OpenViSUS dashboards stream data visualization in as little as 20 minutes, with NSDF Entry Points connecting CHESS to federated storage, compute, and networking.
NSDF Catalog SDSC / UC San Diego

National Science Data Fabric Catalog Grows toward AI-Integrated Scientific Innovation

The NSDF Catalog houses nearly 70 repositories—from geosciences to NASA imagery—with over 1.5 billion records and 75+ petabytes cataloged. A multi-federation effort by SDSC, University of Utah, and University of Tennessee, advancing FAIR data and open science infrastructure.
