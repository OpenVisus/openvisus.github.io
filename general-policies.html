General Policies

Community and Contribution Guidelines. The OpenViSUS ecosystem is governed by our community and is committed to open, safe, and efficient collaboration.

Code of Conduct

The OpenViSUS community strictly adheres to the Contributor Covenant [Source 116]. We are committed to providing a welcoming, harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, or technical background. If you do not have published community participation guidelines, the academic community is often hesitant to adopt the software [Source 116].

Contribution Policy

We strongly encourage community involvement and welcome contributions in the form of bug fixes, documentation, translations, extensions, and core development [Source 54]. All pull requests are reviewed by our core maintainers. By contributing to the OpenViSUS repository, you agree that your contributions will be distributed under the project's OSI-approved BSD-3 Open Source License [Source 205].

Issue Tracking and Support

We utilize GitHub Issues and Pull Requests to promote active participation and feedback [Source 54]. To keep our community workflow efficient and our maintainers from being overwhelmed, we ask all users to follow our published Issue Templates and review the FAQ/Handbook before submitting new tickets [Source 53, 54].
